### Ours
- To much of the web belongs to large corporations
- Corporations use the web to make money for them and make us look longer at their products
- This creates polarisation 
- Large corporations decide what features we can use (make a inline link in linkedIn is not possible like it is on a web page)
- Large corporations spy on us with cookies 
- They can force their values on the web (yes this is politics but if you trust the products you trust their values)
- We need easier ways to publish on the web
